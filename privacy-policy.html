Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AegisPix is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or image data.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Image files or content
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or browser data

2. How Our Service Works

AegisPix operates entirely offline in your browser:

  • All encryption/decryption happens locally on your device
  • No data is uploaded to our servers
  • No internet connection required for core functionality
  • Images are processed using Web Crypto API in your browser

3. Data Security

Your privacy and security are our top priorities:

  • Military-grade encryption: AES-256-GCM algorithm
  • PBKDF2 key derivation: 100,000 iterations for password strengthening
  • Random salts and IVs: Unique for each encryption
  • No data storage: Nothing is saved or cached

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services for data collection or processing. The only external resources are:

  • Web fonts (Segoe UI from Google Fonts)
  • Browser APIs (Web Crypto API, File API)

5. Contact Form

If you choose to contact us:

  • Contact form data is sent via email service (if configured)
  • We only store your message temporarily to respond
  • You can request deletion of your contact information at any time

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our homepage.
